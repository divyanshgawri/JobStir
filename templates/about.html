Who We Are

JobStir is an AI-powered recruitment platform designed to streamline the hiring process for both employers and candidates. It leverages machine learning to intelligently match job openings with the most suitable talent—reducing bias, improving accuracy, and significantly cutting down on manual screening time.

For companies, we offer tools for efficient job posting, smart candidate screening, and seamless interview scheduling. For job seekers, JobStir ensures transparent application processes, AI-driven match scoring, and personalized career insights.
