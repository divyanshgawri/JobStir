Frequently Asked Questions

How does JobStir's AI matching work?

Our AI analyzes over 50 data points from job descriptions and candidate profiles to create accurate match scores. This includes skills, experience, education, and cultural fit indicators.

Is JobStir free to use?

JobStir offers both free and premium plans. Job seekers can use basic features for free, while employers have access to advanced features with our premium plans.

How secure is my data on JobStir?

We take data security seriously. JobStir is GDPR-compliant and uses enterprise-grade security measures to protect your personal and professional information.

Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Yes, you can update your profile, resume, and preferences at any time through your dashboard. We recommend keeping your profile updated for better matches.
